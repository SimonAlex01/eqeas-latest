EQEAS 10-Years Strategic Plan
The vision of a 10-years strategic plan is to have sustainable programs that will contribute to environmental business skills for prosperous communities.

EQEAS 10 Years Strategic Objectives

Over the next ten years (2022-2032), EQEAS will implement interventions/actions under broad intentions outlined below;

  • To contribute on reduction of poverty that occurs as a result of climate change in African communities by the year 2032.
  • To increase on scale of effective program interventions that reach climate change vulnerable communities in their households by year 2032.
  • To contribute towards the reduction of early marriage, domestic violence and regional political conflicts caused by climate change.
  • To empower communities and reduce climate change migrants due to current environmental degradation and water shortage through provision of sustainable utilization of land resources, waste disposal management and enormous tree planting businesses.
  • To add on stock of knowledge and best practices in community development sectors through undertaking operational and technical research.
  • To enhance capacity of EQEAS in communities and sub-partner agencies to deliver combined equitable and quality services by end of 2032 years.
